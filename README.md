# ⚖️ Sanad App – Your Supporting Lawyer

A smart application that provides **legal services and consultations**, combining advanced technology with innovative solutions to make legal support more accessible.

## ✨ Features
- 🤖 AI-powered legal assistant chatbot for quick consultations.
- 🧮 Legal calculator for topics like inheritance.
- 📝 Request legal document drafting (contracts, memos, etc.).
- 📅 Book legal consultation sessions with certified lawyers.

## 🛠️ Requirements
- User account creation.
- Verified lawyer accounts (with bar license).
- Data protection & confidentiality.
- High speed and accuracy in responses.

## 🗂️ Project Management
- Agile / Scrum methodologies for development.
- Tools: Jira, Trello, MS Planner.


## ⚠️ Potential Risks
- Unverified lawyer licenses.
- Issues with financial transactions.
- Lack of training data for AI model.

## 🚀 Growth Opportunities
- Add in-person appointment booking.
- Enable power of attorney & court representation services.
